Governments from around the globe sought to find a way to save humanity from being erased from time. The governments broke up the volunteers into teams and scattered them across the globe hoping for one of them to find a solution. One of the teams undertook a desperate gamble of information and engineering to attempt to create and electromagnetic shield which would isolate those inside the shield from the Time-Storm. As the storm arrived all hope seemed lost but in the final hours with only a few remaining the shield hummed to life. 


With limited supplies, power, and people and the storm seemingly gaining strength will they survive or will all be lost.
